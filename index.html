About Us

We are Destov International

Destov International is a Mumbai based hub for exports of the top quality fresh fruits and vegetables. We procure Fresh Fruits and Vegetables directly from our trained farmers which reduces the overall pricing of our products. Our products include world famous Nashik Onions, the Luscious Alphonso Mangoes, Pomegranates, Bananas,you name it.

Why Us

WHY DESTOV INTERNATIONAL ?

We are more focused on quality and it is much better to stop where we see quality failure. We offer Live Packaging Video feature which will help you to ensure that the right quality and quantity is being packed and loaded into the containers. We are strategically located in Mumbai, which facilitates international trade through its largest port of Nhava Sheva resulting into reduced lead time. We have network of over 200+ farmers to handpick best quality fruits and vegetables. We have Latest cold storage Technological facilities in Nashik which retains and enhances the quality of our products.

With the integration of science of packaging and stuffing, we manage to pack your orders by our own hands which further ensures the filtration of low quality materials and less perishability of the products. We do Standard Packaging as per APEDA Guidelines. We can offer customized packaging as per the client’s requirement. Our farmers are our pride and we can’t thank them enough to be a part in our journey. With Destov, Your Idea of satisfaction can never be compromised. We look forward to serve the globe, extending our vertical beyond agro commodities, in the near future.
